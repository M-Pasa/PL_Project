## Retrospective

**What I would change.** I would lift `<let-decl>` to a prefix section from day one rather than placing it inside `<rules-section>`. **Hardest design decision.** Whether to model `Rate(D‚ÇÅ,D‚ÇÇ)` as a separate kind or as a derived result of `Quantity D‚ÇÅ / Quantity D‚ÇÇ`. The kind (what I chose) costs one asymmetry ‚Äî `plateStep / 1 week` is a static error ‚Äî but keeps the type universe finite and avoids the question of whether `Mass √ó Time` is the inverse, which is nonsense in the domain.
